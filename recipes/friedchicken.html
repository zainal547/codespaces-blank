Fried Chicken

Bahan-bahan

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil for frying

Instruksi

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Dredge each chicken piece in the flour mixture and shake off excess.
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Fry the chicken pieces for 6-8 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite sides.
