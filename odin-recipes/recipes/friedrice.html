Bahan-bahan

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of chili powder (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion, and saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes until they are tender.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, black pepper, and chili powder (if using). Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  5. If desired, add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the skillet and stir until heated through.
  6. Serve the fried rice hot, garnished with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired.
